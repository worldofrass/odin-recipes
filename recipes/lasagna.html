Lasagna Recipe

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. It's a hearty and comforting dish that's perfect for family dinners or special occasions. The recipe is easy to follow and yields a delicious, restaurant-quality lasagna that everyone will love!

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (24 oz) jar marinara sauce
  • 1 (15 oz) container ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and cook until softened.
  4. Add the ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  5. Stir in the marinara sauce and simmer for 10 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce. Layer 4 lasagna noodles on top.
  7. Spread half of the ricotta cheese over the noodles, followed by a third of the mozzarella cheese.
  8. Repeat the layers: meat sauce, noodles, ricotta, and mozzarella.
  9. Top with the remaining noodles, meat sauce, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheese.
  10. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until bubbly and golden.
  11. Let the lasagna cool for 10 minutes before serving.
